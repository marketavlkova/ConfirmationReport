\chapter*{Objective}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Objective}

The objectives of this study are:

\begin{enumerate}
\item Explore genotypic and phenotypic variation in promoters of various genes among environmental isolates of \tax{E. coli}.

\item Compare natural variation in transcriptional responses with variation created by random mutagenesis.

\end{enumerate}
